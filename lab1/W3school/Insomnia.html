How to Deal with Insomnia

What is Insomnia? Tips for Better Sleep

What is Insomnia?

Insomnia is a common sleep disorder that makes it hard to fall asleep, stay asleep, or get restful sleep. Here are some quick facts:

  • Difficulty falling asleep: Lying awake for hours.
  • Frequent waking: Waking up multiple times during the night.
  • Feeling tired: Even after sleeping, you feel unrefreshed.
  • Impact: Insomnia can affect your mood, health, and productivity.

Tips for Better Sleep

Below are some methods to tackle insomnia and improve your sleep quality:

Tip Description
Establish a sleep schedule Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends.
Limit screen time Avoid using phones, computers, or watching TV at least an hour before bed.
Relaxation techniques Try reading, meditation, or deep breathing exercises to relax your mind.
